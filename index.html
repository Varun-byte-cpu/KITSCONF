About the Conference

The International Conference on Intelligent Systems, Computing and Communication Technologies (ICISCT) aims to bring together leading researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and innovators to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of intelligent systems, computing paradigms, and emerging communication technologies. This conference serves as a dynamic platform to foster collaboration, encourage interdisciplinary research, and explore transformative technologies that are shaping the future of smart and connected societies.

With rapid advancements in areas such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, cloud and edge computing, IoT, 5G/6G communication, and cyber-physical systems, ICISCT provides a timely forum for discussing cutting-edge innovations and real-world applications. The conference will feature keynote addresses by eminent speakers, technical paper presentations, panel discussions, workshops, and opportunities for networking and collaboration.

ICISCT welcomes contributions that address both theoretical advancements and practical solutions, paving the way toward sustainable, intelligent, and resilient systems for the digital era.
